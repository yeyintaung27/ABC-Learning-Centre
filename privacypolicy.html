Privacy Policy

ABC Learning Center Institute takes the privacy of its students, staff, and partners very seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information that we may obtain through our website, services, or other interactions with our users.

Types of Personal Information Collected

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information (e.g., name, address, email, phone number)
  • Demographic information (e.g., age, gender, education, occupation)
  • Payment information (e.g., credit card or other financial information)
  • Course or program information (e.g., course history, program preferences)
  • Other information relevant to the services we offer or the use of our website (e.g., feedback or comments)

Collection and Use of Personal Information

We use personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services and website.
  • To communicate with you about your account, courses, and programs.
  • To send you information about updates, promotions, and other events.
  • To process payments for our services.
  • To comply with legal requirements, such as tax and accounting regulations.

Disclosure of Personal Information

We will not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties without your prior consent. However, we may share your information with service providers who assist us with our business operations.
